AI PING PONG GAME











Instructions

  1. Keep your laptop straight.



  2. Click on the 'PLAY GAME' button to start the game.



  3. Use your finger to move the paddle.



  4. Score Points and win the game.



  5. As more you score , the speed of the game will increase to:- 3 to 3.5, 3.5 to 4, 4 to 4.5, 4.5 to 5 and so on...
  6. If you lose, thwn click on the 'Restart' button to again play the game.
